paper violin

Inspired by the Studio Ghibli film Whisper of the Heart, I created a playable acoustic violin made out of paper, popsicle sticks and white glue. I designed and built all parts of the violin except the strings, tuning pegs, bridge and tailpiece.

paper sword

A replica sword modeled after the anime Sword Art Online. The sword is hand made and the only materials used were paper, popsicle sticks, and white glue. The sword is also very structurally sound, it won't cut anything, but can withstand multiple swings at full force against hard objects.

rilakuma wall display

A large scale geometric wall display of the popular Japanese character Rilakuma. The display is about three feet tall and four feet wide. It is composed of hundreds of pieces of paper, folded into pyramid shapes.

chinese shadow puppet

Replica ancient Chinese shadow theater puppet. Manilla folder was soaked in oil to increase transparency, and then cut into the shape of the puppets appendages. The puppet was then painted, the joints were sewn together, and rods were attached.

wooden rice scoop

Handmade wooden rice scoop. The rice scoop was designed with an asymmetrical paddle to increase ease of scooping when used with the right hand.

__hi Typeface

A condensed typeface made from a restricted grid of triangles. It was designed to be used when horizontal space is limited. The typeface features the alphanumeric characters as well as a small array of special characters.

ilytea

A sidewalk vending bubble tea stand I created over the Summer of 2018. I designed the polar bear logo as well as the logotype for the banner. The logo was found on the cup stickers, menu, and was stenciled on the inside of the canopy.

motion graphics lyric video

Motion graphics lyric video I made for a popular cover singer on YouTube. The only assets that were given to me were the background image and the two characters. The video has been viewed around 184,000 times.

motion graphics final project

Final project in my motion graphics class. The video was based on a stamp from the Netherlands. The shapes that the dots and lines create were based off of the geographical outlines of all the major cities in the Netherlands. The colors of dots, background and lines, were taken from the each cities respective flag.
