Paper Violin

A playable violin made from paper and popsicle sticks. The aesthetic and structural design are both completely original. The project took around 100 hours to complete.

Pop Up Microphone

A book cover for a collection of interviews. The microphone makes use of original paper mechanics that I invented.

Paper Embossment Poster

A frame and poster of a popular character. To make the poster I created a system that allows for precise multi-level embossment.

Pop Up Emoji

A pop up of the recently popular pleading emoji. To make the card I created a system that can be used to create infinitely stackable 2.5D pop ups.

Pop Up Boston Sidewalk

A pop up inspired from Boston's architecture. To make the pop up I created a system that allows for multi level, zero friction movement.

Rotterdam Stationary

A collection of paper products featuring my rebrand of Rotterdam. The concept of my rebrand was "Open Everything" which is why the pamphlet and envelope open at the 'TT' in Rotterdam. I also designed a pop up of Rotterdam's most famous bridge the Erasmusbrug.

Paper Sword

A replica sword made from paper. The sword can withstand hard impacts and the project took about 80 hours to complete.

T Shirt Packaging

T shirt packaging for my clothing brand, baby polar bear.
