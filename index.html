Memory Pop Up Book

A pop up recreation of my best friend's favorite memory with his girlfriend. The pop up features complex kirigami architecture and multiple figures raising away from the crease. The pop up has no visible structural tabs, the whole pop up moves through hidden mechanics.

Rom Com Pop Up Book

A pop up recreation of a typical scene from a romantic comedy. The book features a collapsing spine, no visible structural tabs and hidden mechanics.

Adidas Pop Up

A multilayered pop up of the adidas logo. The pop up features a minimal style and exhibits how paper engineering can be used for promotional advertising and branding.

Paper Violin

A playable violin made from paper. The project was an independent study completed at the University of Connecticut. The aesthetic and structural design are both original. The project took around 200 hours to complete.

Pop Up Microphone

A pop up microphone incorporated into a book cover. The book is a handbound collection of interviews. The book also features custom embossment on the front and back cover.

Paper Embossment Poster

A frame and poster of a popular character. To emboss the poster I created a system that allows for precise multilevel embossment.

Pop Up Emoji

A multilayered pop up of the recently popular pleading emoji.

Rotterdam Stationary

A collection of paper products featuring my rebrand of Rotterdam. The concept of my rebrand was "Open Everything" which is why the pamphlet and envelope open at the 'TT' in Rotterdam. I also designed a pop up of Rotterdam's most famous bridge, the Erasmusbrug.

Paper Sword

A replica sword made from paper. The detail and coloring are all done with paper. The sword is structurally sound and can withstand hard impacts. The project took about 100 hours to complete.

T Shirt Packaging

T shirt packaging for my clothing brand, baby polar bear.
